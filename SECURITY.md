# Vulnerability Disclosure Policy

As an independent developer, I value the work of security researchers in keeping the ecosystem safe. If you believe you have found a security vulnerability affecting this site, please report it privately following the guidelines below.

## Reporting a Vulnerability
* Please email your findings to **security@sibikaitharath.com**.
* To protect sensitive details, please encrypt your email using the PGP key found at `https://sibikaitharath.com/pgp-key.txt`.

## Guidelines & Safe Harbor
If you make a good-faith effort to comply with this policy, I promise to:
* Not pursue legal action or involve law enforcement regarding your research.
* Work with you to understand and validate the issue quickly.
* Not share your personal data or disclosure details with third parties without your permission.

## Out of Scope
The following activities are strictly prohibited and outside the scope of safe harbor:
* Denial of Service (DoS/DDoS) attacks against the Cloudflare edge network or infrastructure.
* Automated spam tools or high-frequency brute-force scanning.
* Social engineering, phishing, or physical security attacks.

Thank you for helping keep the web secure!
